Welcome to the **Circuit Simulations** repository! 🚀

This repository is a part of my academic journey, where I simulate and analyze various electronic circuits that I study during my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). It serves as a practical playground to understand theoretical concepts through hands-on simulation tools.

## 📁 What's Inside?

Currently, the repository includes simulations for:
- **Coherent Detector**
  - `LTspice_Coherent_detector/`: Simulation files for LTspice.
  - `Proteus_Coherent_detector/`: Simulation files for Proteus.
  - `Schmitt_trigger/`: Simulation files for LTspice.

*More circuits will be added as I progress through my coursework!*

## 🤝 Let's Collaborate!

This project is **100% Open Source**, and I am very open to collaboration! 

If you are a fellow ECE student, electronics enthusiast, or hobbyist, you are more than welcome to contribute. Did you simulate a cool circuit on LTspice, Proteus, Multisim, or any other software? 

**I encourage you to add your own simulations!**
1. Fork the repository
2. Create a new folder for your circuit simulation
3. Add your files with a brief explanation (maybe a mini-README inside your folder!)
4. Submit a Pull Request!

Let's learn together and build a helpful resource for everyone studying electronics. 💡
